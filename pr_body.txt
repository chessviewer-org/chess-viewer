Closes #136

## What & why

Fixed a layout issue on mobile where `box-content border-[3px]` caused the Export Preview canvas wrapper to overflow the viewport horizontally, cutting off the right-side border. Moved the border rendering to a `pointer-events-none absolute inset-0` overlay that draws inside the bounds without altering the element's layout width. Also bumped the package version to `v6.0.1`.
